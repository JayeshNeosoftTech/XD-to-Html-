About VIDC

VIDARBHA IRRIGATION DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION ACT, 1997. First published, after having received the assent of the Governor, in the “Maharashtra Government Gazette” on 28th April 1997. An Act to make special provisions for mobilisation of resources for completion of some ongoing irrigation protects in time bound manner, for promotion and operation of the said irrigation projects in Vidarbha region and command area development and schemes for generation of hydro-electric energy to harness the water of Godavari and Tapi Rivers allocated to the State of Maharashtra under the Water Disputes Tribunal Award and other allied and incidental activities including flood control by establishing the Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation. It was expedient to make special provisions for promotion and operation of some irrigation projects in Vidarbha region.
